# Prepare image frame for dynamic content

[⟵](../README.md)

![Auto fill image frame](../assets/ImageFrame/01-autofill-image-frame.gif)

To ensure that images are always visible within the frame when exporting, it is necessary to configure the properties so that the image always stays within the frame without being stretched.

Be aware, however, that if you want a specific part of the image to be visible, some post-processing may be required after export.
